Who am I ?

A Talented Cloud Engineer

Graduated from Telkom University majoring in Telecommunication Engineering. Currently working as Cloud Core Network Engineer at Huawei Indonesia specialized on 4G/5G Packet Core Network. Have experience working as engineer in several Indonesian operators such as XL Axiata and Indosat Ooredoo.

I am familiar with several cloud platforms such as Google Cloud (GCP), Amazon (AWS), and Huawei Cloud with FusionSphere Openstack environment. Linux environment is my working tools everyday and I have a lot experience in programming using Python, Bash, and Excel VBA to suport my work. I also have experience with open-source software such as Open5GS and srsRAN for 4G/5G Network Simulation.

I am also familiar with virtualization and containerization technologies such as Docker and Kubernetes. I have experience in deploying microservice applications using VM and/or Container method, alao familiar with database management both SQL and NoSQL databases. I have good understanding of computer networking especially in routing and switching, also understand about network security and very familiar with network monitoring and troubleshooting tools.

I am a fast learner, and I am always eager to learn new things. I am also a team player, and I have good communication skills. I am looking for new opportunities to grow and develop my skills in the field of Cloud Computing and Software Engineering.
